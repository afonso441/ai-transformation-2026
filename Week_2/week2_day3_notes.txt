Week 2 Day 3: Combining Async + APIs + AI

Architecture:
1. User asks about weather in a city
2. Async fetch weather data (fast, parallel if multiple cities)
3. Pass weather data to Claude for interpretation
4. Claude analyzes: temperature, humidity, conditions
5. Claude provides: what to wear, activity recommendations, travel tips

Why this pattern matters:
- Async = Speed (fetch multiple cities at once)
- API = Real data (not static/fake)
- AI = Intelligence (turn raw data into insights)

Real-world applications:
- Stock analysis (fetch prices, AI analyzes trends)
- IoT monitoring (fetch sensor data, AI detects anomalies)
- News aggregation (fetch articles, AI summarizes)
- Health tracking (fetch vitals, AI gives recommendations)

This is the foundation of 90% of AI applications!
